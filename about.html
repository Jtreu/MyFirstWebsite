About

Hi! Chances are, if you stumbled upon this website, you're on of the few people to take interest. Congratulations! Unfortunately, I don't really have a prize for you, but what I can do is tell you about myself.

I'm a 20-something year old college student going to the University of Central Missouri in Warrensburg. I'm currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, aiming towards software design. An ideal i hold close to my heart is that humans should be free to learn and do almost anything they want through technology and the internet. This is especially why I love the open-source model of design, and even more so games that give you the freedom to do whatever you please. I hope that before I kick the bucket I can better people's lives through my favorite medium.

I enjoy all sorts of things. Mainly gaming, programming, biking, music, and coffee that's so jacked up it tastes like pickled battery acid (gotta stay awake somehow!).

If you'd like to follow me on twitter, my username is @dudetreu.
